2026 Masters Payout Table
2026 Masters Tournament official prize money by finishing position ($20,000,000 total purse). When multiple players share a position, prize money is pooled across all tied spots and split equally — so a 5-way tie for 3rd pays each player the average of the 3rd–7th place prizes, not the full 3rd-place amount.
How earnings are calculated
Each team picks 4 golfers. During the tournament, each golfer's projected payout is based on their current leaderboard position using the Masters payout table.

A team's total projected earnings = the sum of all 4 golfers' projected payouts.

If a golfer misses the cut, they earn $0 for the pool — they're done.

Tie-breaker: When two teams have identical projected earnings, the tie-breaker guess (total strokes under par) is used. Closest to the actual final score without going over wins.
